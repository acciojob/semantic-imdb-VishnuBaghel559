Summary

"Ramayan" is an epic saga of Lord Rama's journey to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana. It portrays themes of duty, honor, and righteousness, deeply rooted in Indian mythology and spiritual values.

Actors

Arun Govil as Lord Rama, Deepika Chikhalia as Sita, and Sunil Lahri as Lakshman brought these characters to life in the iconic adaptation. Their performances remain etched in the hearts of millions.

Favorite Quote

“Dharmo rakshati rakshitah” – Dharma protects those who protect it.
